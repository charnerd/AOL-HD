# Aquatics on Land: Hydra-Deficient (AOL-HD)

AOL-HD is a 2D minigame created on the Unity Game Engine using C#. 
Game Link: https://play.unity.com/api/v1/games/game/6297e0bc-5b93-46df-abb6-20593f5e840f/build/latest/frame

**Movement and Controls**
1) WASD/Arrow Keys - move up and down or left and right
2) E - interact with items
3) Mouse-Click - choose buttons in the main menu and game over screen

In this game, you play as a fish trying to survive on land for as long as possible. To help you, there are water sources such as puddles, raindrops, and non-natural sources such as coke bottles to keep your health up. While keeping your health up, you can interact with different items to increase your score (seashells, coins, skulls, etc.). Additionally, there are weather events such as heatwaves and thunderstorms that can either greatly decrease or increase your health (you also get a bonus to your score if you survive these events). Also, you are not allowed to interact with water sources when these weather events occur. There is also a castle somewhere on the island with a queen, that will give a huge bonus to your score.

**Here are some things to remember:**
1) Your health goes down by 1 per second
2) Natural water sources (puddles, raindrops) will disappear if interacted with twice, while non-natural water sources (coke bottles) will disappear after on interaction.
3) The score multiplier increases with the more items you interact with
4) One weather event will spawn every 30 seconds (**Heatwaves** (10 seconds) decrease your score, **Thunderstorms** (8 seconds) regenerate your health).

**Tips and Tricks**
1) Be resourceful with the water on land
2) Keep your health above 30 at least so that you are able to get a random event to either drain your health or regenerate it.
